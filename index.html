About US

Living in an apartment has never been so good. Some look for apartments in a prime location while other look for comfortable living. In these garden style apartment homes, you’ll feel tucked away in a secluded neighborhood. It won’t be until you drive onto the main road that you’ll realize that you’re close to everything important.

This desirable location is only minutes away from Rutgers, Middlesex Community College, Menlo Park Mall, downtown New Brunswick, St. Peter's University and Robert Wood Johnson University. Not to mention some of New Jersey’s best dining, shopping, and entertainment. You won’t have to go far for everything you need!

Middlesex County is a prime location for commuters. Less than an hour away from New York City or Philadelphia, and easy access to Rt 18, 27, I-95 and the Gardenstate Parkway, as well as express buses and the New Brunswick train station, it is ideal for living in comfort without extending your commute.

Within the secure gated neighborhood, you’ll find manicured green lawns and cozy garden areas. With seasonally landscaped flowerbeds scattered through the community, this place feels like home before you even enter your apartment.

To experience the true luxury of living close to everywhere and secluded from everything, experience Windy Willows Apartment Homes as your new home.
